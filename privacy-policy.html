Privacy Policy

PriceVision | Last updated: January 2025

Summary: PriceVision does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

Information We Do NOT Collect

PriceVision is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers for advertising
  • Browsing history or app usage patterns

Information We Access

To provide our service, PriceVision accesses:

  • Market Data: Publicly available pricing/market data from third-party sources/APIs (public endpoints). This contains no personal user information.
  • Subscription Status: Anonymous subscription verification through RevenueCat to determine Pro feature access. This does not include any personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

PriceVision uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat processes transactions anonymously. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store: For payment processing. Apple handles all payment information. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Third-Party Data APIs: For retrieving public pricing/market data. We only access publicly available information.

Data Storage

PriceVision stores minimal data locally on your device:

  • Your watchlist selections (stored only on your device)
  • App preferences (stored only on your device)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

PriceVision is not intended for use by children under 17. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
